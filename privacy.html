Faith Bag — Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 27, 2025

The Faith Bag (“the App”) is built by an individual developer. This policy explains what data the App uses and why.

Data We Collect

  • iCloud Key-Value storage: The App stores your preferences and app state (e.g., streak count, last draw time, chosen emoji, reminder choices) in your iCloud Key-Value Store so they sync across your devices. This data is tied to your iCloud account and not shared with the developer.
  • Core Data (on-device): Your takeaways are stored on your device. If you enable iCloud/CloudKit syncing, your data may sync via Apple’s services between your devices.
  • Notifications: If you allow notifications, we store your reminder preferences locally and schedule local notifications on your device.

Payments

Tips (In-App Purchases): Apple processes payments. The App does not receive or store your payment details.

Analytics / Tracking

The App does not use third-party analytics SDKs or advertising trackers.

Data Sharing

The App does not share your personal data with third parties.

Your Choices

  • You can disable iCloud sync and notifications at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete your data by removing the App. Any iCloud KV items will be removed per Apple’s retention schedule.
