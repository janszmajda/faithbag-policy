Faith Bag — Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 27, 2025

Faith Bag (“the App”) is built by an individual developer. This policy explains what data the App uses and why.

Data We Collect

  • iCloud Key-Value storage: The App stores limited preferences and app state (e.g., streak count, last draw time, chosen emoji, reminder choices) in your iCloud Key-Value Store so they sync across your devices. These items are tied to your iCloud account and are not shared with the developer.
  • Core Data (on-device) & CloudKit (optional): Your takeaways are stored on your device. If you enable iCloud/CloudKit syncing, your takeaways sync privately via Apple’s services between your devices. Records reside in your private iCloud database.
  • Widgets & App Groups: Widgets read your on-device settings via Apple’s App Group storage for the app; this does not send data to third parties.
  • Notifications: If you allow notifications, the App stores your reminder preferences locally and schedules local notifications on your device. If remote notifications are enabled in the future, Apple may issue a device push token to deliver messages; the App does not link tokens to your identity.

Payments

Tips (In-App Purchases): Apple processes payments. The App does not receive or store your payment details.

Analytics / Tracking

The App does not use third-party analytics SDKs or advertising trackers.

Data Sharing

The App does not share your personal data with third parties.

Data Deletion & Your Choices

  • You can disable iCloud sync and notifications at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can remove local data via the in-app “Delete Data” option or by deleting the App. iCloud Key-Value items are cleared by the App and removed per Apple’s retention schedule. If CloudKit sync is enabled, takeaways can be deleted from your iCloud via the in-app option.
